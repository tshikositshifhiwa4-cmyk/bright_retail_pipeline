05 — Business Logic to Remember
The formulas that show up in every query

Line Amount

Line_Amount = Unit_Price × Quantity
Line_Amount = Unit_Price × Quantity × (1 − Discount%)

Use the discount version whenever a line-level discount applies. This is the cost of that individual line item before any invoice-level tax or discount.

Reorder Threshold

WHERE Stock_On_Hand <= Reorder_Threshold

Stock at or below the reorder point needs restocking now. Split into = threshold (needs reorder) vs < threshold (overdue, urgent) for a sharper answer.

Discount Impact

Revenue Lost = Σ (Unit_Price × Qty × Discount%)

Find what % of revenue is being given away in discounts, and whether it's concentrated in specific stores, products, or tiers.

Revenue & Profit

Profit = Revenue − (Cost_Price × Quantity)

Revenue comes from Line_Amount; profit needs cost_price brought in from the product/supplier side.
